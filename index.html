Oleksandra Cheipesh

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid. I focus on the study of health and labor economics. My reserach examines how health influences economic outcomes and how labor market opportunities affect health. I am also interested in the drivers of gender norms and stereotypes. To address these questions, I primarily employ quasi-experimental methods.
